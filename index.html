About Stonebranch Capital

Stonebranch Capital LLC is a privately held parent company dedicated to owning, operating, and supporting a portfolio of independent ventures. From startup services to scalable operations, our mission is to empower each business we own with the structure, resources, and flexibility to thrive.

With a focus on growth, strategy, and long-term sustainability, Stonebranch Capital exists to build businesses the right way — from the ground up.
